Ooo! Shiny!

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Mine your first nugget of ore with a pickaxe.

Notes: Placing an ore block and then mining it also grants the achievement. The following ores count for this achievement: Iron, Copper, Gold, Silver, Demonite, Hellstone, Cobalt, Mythril, Adamantite, Tin, Lead, Tungsten, Platinum, Crimtane, Chlorophyte, Palladium, Orichalcum, Titanium. The following ores do not count for the achievement: Meteorite, Obsidian, Luminite. The ore in the photo is Copper Ore, and the pickaxe appears to be Tin or Iron.
