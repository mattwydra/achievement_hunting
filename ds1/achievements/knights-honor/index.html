Knight's Honor

Acquire all rare weapons

Very Hard

Overview

The Knight's Honor achievement requires you to collect all rare weapons in Dark Souls. This is one of the most challenging achievements to obtain as it requires multiple playthroughs and careful planning.

You'll need to reach at least NG++ (third playthrough) to acquire all the boss soul weapons, as you need three souls from Sif to craft all the weapons that require the Soul of Sif.

Required Weapons

You need to collect the following weapon categories:

  • All boss soul weapons
  • All tail-cut weapons
  • All unique weapons from enemies and locations
  • All covenant reward weapons

Strategy

To efficiently obtain this achievement:

  1. Plan your covenant progress across playthroughs
  2. Never consume boss souls - save them for crafting
  3. Keep a checklist to track which weapons you've already obtained
  4. Focus on tail cuts during your first encounter with each dragon/boss
  5. Save Sif's soul for NG++ when you can craft all three weapons

NG++ Requirements

You will need to reach at least NG++ (third playthrough) to get this achievement because:

  • You need three souls of Sif to craft all three weapons: Greatsword of Artorias, Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed), and Great Shield of Artorias
  • You need two souls of Gwyn for the Greatshield of Artorias and the Greatsword of Artorias
