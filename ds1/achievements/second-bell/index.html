Ring the Bell (Quelaag's Domain)

Ring Bell of Awakening in Quelaag's domain.

Medium

Overview

Survive the poisonous depths of Blighttown to challenge Quelaag and ring the second Bell of Awakening.

This is the second main bell required to unlock Sen’s Fortress. The fight itself is manageable if you're patient, but reaching Quelaag’s Domain can be more dangerous than the boss due to Blighttown’s toxic hazards and tough enemies.

Additional Information

Information on boss fight:

  1. Boss Location: Quelaag’s Domain, at the base of Blighttown.
  2. Quelaag Fight Mechanics:
    • Quelaag is a half-woman, half-spider boss who deals heavy fire damage and explosive AoEs.
    • Fight Strategy:
      • Watch for lava spits — they persist on the ground and restrict movement.
      • Stay behind or to the side of her spider body to avoid many of her attacks.
      • Use a shield with high fire resistance (e.g., Black Knight Shield) if you're blocking.
      • Light armor or rolling builds should focus on dodging her melee swipes and lava zones.
      Note: You can summon Maneater Mildred if you defeat her invasion near the swamp bonfire.
    • Fire resistance and poison-curing items (like Purple Moss) are highly recommended for the lead-up through Blighttown.
    • After defeating Quelaag, the second Bell of Awakening is located just past the boss fog gate, guarded by a stationary character.
