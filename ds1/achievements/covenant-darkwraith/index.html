Darkwraith

Discover the Darkwraith Covenant.

Hard

Overview

This is one of the easiest covenants to miss and requires careful planning to join. The Darkwraiths are PvP-focused invaders who serve Darkstalker Kaathe.

To join, you must defeat the Four Kings *before* placing the Lordvessel, and *without speaking to Frampt*. Kaathe will then appear and offer the covenant.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Progress through the game until you defeat Ornstein and Smough and receive the Lordvessel.
  2. Do NOT speak to Frampt or place the Lordvessel at Firelink Altar.
  3. Head to New Londo Ruins (below Firelink Shrine).
  4. Drain the water in the ruins:
    • Talk to Ingward on the roof of a building in the ruins (use ladder + stairs).
    • He gives you the Key to the Seal.
    • Unlock the door in the lower area and pull the lever to drain the water.
  5. Prepare for the Four Kings:
    • You must wear the Covenant of Artorias ring (from killing Sif) to survive in the Abyss.
    • Equip the ring before entering the boss fog or you will die instantly.
  6. Defeat the Four Kings boss.
    • Time is of the essence â€” if you take too long, multiple Kings will spawn simultaneously.
    • Stay aggressive to prevent being overwhelmed.
  7. After the fight, Darkstalker Kaathe will appear.
    • Speak with him to join the Darkwraith Covenant.
    • He will also allow you to place the Lordvessel afterward.
    • Note: Once you talk to Kaathe, Frampt will disappear from Firelink Shrine permanently.

Rewards

  • Join the covenant to receive the Dark Hand weapon (absorbs humanity from NPCs and players).
  • Rank up by giving Kaathe Humanity to receive Red Eye Orbs and the Darkwraith armor set.
  • Unlocks PvP invasion mechanic via Red Eye Orb (infinite use).

Warning

Placing the Lordvessel with Frampt (even just speaking to him and falling in) will lock you out of this covenant for the rest of the game.
