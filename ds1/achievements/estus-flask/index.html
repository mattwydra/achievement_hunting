Estus Flask

Acquire Estus Flask

Easy

Overview

The Estus Flask achievement can be somewhat tricky to obtain, but is necessary to progress.

After collecting the first bonfire, enter through the massive doors that lie ahead. Through here, you will meet your first boss, and first major challenge. Seeing as you will do very little damage and have no flasks, it is highly unlikely that you will be able to defeat the Asylum Demon. After entering the room, you will find an open gate at the back left side of the room. Enter through here, and continue with the logical progression. Upon reaching a set of stairs, there will be an enemy that rolls a metal ball down them in an attempt to kill you. If you can avoid dying here (either tanking the damage or baiting out the ball), then you can turn around and notice that the ball has opened a hole in the wall that was once closed off. In here, you will find Oscar of Astora, who will tell you of your fate. At the end of one of my favorite monologues, he will offer you an "undead favorite", the estus flask. Upon receiving the flask, you will unlock this achievement.

Additional Information

How Estus Flasks work in Dark Souls:

  1. Recover some HP, dependent on the amount of times you have reinforced your flask.
  2. Refill when resting at bonfires.
  3. You start with 5 flasks, but this number can go up almost infinitely. There are a few ways to increase this number:
    • Resting at a bonfire where there is a Fire Keeper present will net you 10 flasks.
    • Kindling the bonfire will net you more flasks:
      • Regular bonfire: 5
      • Upgrading once (or Fire Keeper present): 10
      • Upgraded twice (or once with Fire Keeper): 15
      • Upgraded three times (or twice with Fire Keeper): 20

    • If someone in another world kindles the bonfire you most recently rested at, you'll get an additional flask.
    • Slaying NPC invaders can sometimes reward you with extra flasks.
