Reach Lordran

Arrive in Lordran.

Easy

Overview

This achievement is granted upon leaving the starting area (Northern Undead Asylum), and landing at Lordran's Firelink Shrine.

Lordran is the area wherein our journey will unfold. We start in firelink shrine after being dropped off by a massive bird. In order to find Lordran, we must approach the end of the cliff after opening the double doors out of the arena where we defeated the asylum demon. This will begin a cutscene where we end at Lordran.

Additional Information

Information on Northern Undead Asylum:

  1. It is possible to defeat the Asylum Demon before getting the longsword, but completely optional. There is a gate on the left side toward the back of the room that opens once you enter the room. Go through there in order to progress. Eventually, you will see a fog wall where you can plunging attack the Asylum Demon in order to do massive damage at the beginning of the fight. But, don't linger too long. If you take too long to drop, he will jump and break the ledge, dealing massive damage to you.
  2. Upon defeating the Asylum Demon, go through the closed doors (walk up to them + interact). You will be able to unlock them with the key dropped by Asylum Demon. Continue straight out the doors until you find the edge of the cliff. Walk up toward the edge, and the cutscene will start.
  3. Return to the Northern Undead Asylum is possible. I don't want to reveal spoilers, but there will be a link eventually found here
