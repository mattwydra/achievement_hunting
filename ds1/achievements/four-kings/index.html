Defeat the Four Kings

Defeat the Four Kings, inheritors of souls.

Medium

Overview

This fight takes place in the Abyss and is one of the more punishing boss encounters in the game, especially if your damage output is low. It's also critical for Darkwraith covenant access, depending on the order of events in your playthrough.

WARNING: SPOILERS! For more information on lore and their connection to the Darkwraiths, see here.

Preparation

  • You must have the Covenant of Artorias ring (from defeating Sif) to survive the Abyss.
    • Full guide on getting the ring can be found here
  • High DPS is recommended — the longer the fight goes, the more Kings spawn.
  • Bring fast weapons, a strong shield or armor, and high poise if possible.

Strategy

  1. Equip the Covenant of Artorias before dropping into the Abyss — you will die instantly without it.
  2. Once in the Abyss, the first King spawns after a few seconds. Stay aggressive.
  3. Deal as much damage as possible:
    • The Four Kings share one health bar, but new ones spawn on a timer (and I'm fairly certain the total that you fight can exceed four if you're not quick enough).
    • Too slow = multiple Kings at once = difficult fight.
    • Strong melee builds usually fare better than ranged builds here.
  4. Be cautious of their grab attack and magic AoE — it can deal massive damage if unblocked or dodged poorly.
  5. Once the last King is dead, a bonfire will reveal itself (and you might meet Kaathe, if requirements are met. Be wary of spoilers when looking into this).

Tips

  • Fast roll builds can dodge most melee attacks if timed well.
  • Two-handed weapons or buffs like Power Within/Resin can help burst down each King quickly.
  • Summons are allowed, but be warned: multiple players means Kings have more health.
