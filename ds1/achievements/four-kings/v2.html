Defeat the Four Kings

Defeat the Four Kings, inheritors of New Londo

Hard

Overview

The Four Kings are one of the four required Lord Soul bosses that must be defeated after placing the Lordvessel. They reside in the Abyss beneath New Londo Ruins, and the fight can only be accessed after specific requirements are met.

How to Access the Fight

  • Acquire the Lordvessel from Gwynevere in Anor Londo.
  • Important: Do not place the Lordvessel with Kingseeker Frampt if you want to join the Darkwraith covenant later. Instead, go to New Londo Ruins and speak to Ingward on the rooftop after defeating the Capra Demon if you haven’t already.
  • Ingward will drain the water once you have the Lordvessel.
  • Head down to the sealed door that leads to the Abyss.
  • You must have the Covenant of Artorias ring (obtained from Sif) equipped to survive in the Abyss.

Boss Strategy: The Four Kings

  1. The Four Kings fight is deceptive — it starts with one, but more spawn over time regardless of your progress. Fast DPS is key to surviving this encounter.
  2. Stay close to the boss to avoid wide sweeping attacks and use powerful melee or magic.
  3. Bring armor or rings that increase magic resistance, such as the Havel’s Ring or Magic Stoneplate Ring.
  4. The fight takes place in total darkness with no solid ground, so movement can be disorienting.
  5. If you defeat them without speaking to Frampt after acquiring the Lordvessel, you will meet Darkstalker Kaathe and unlock the path to the Darkwraith Covenant.

Lore: The Fall of New Londo

The Four Kings were once noble rulers of New Londo, entrusted with fragments of a Lord Soul by Gwyn. However, they succumbed to the temptation of the Abyss, seduced by the serpent Darkstalker Kaathe.

Their corruption doomed the city. In a desperate act to contain the spread of the Abyss, New Londo was flooded, sacrificing countless lives in the process.

Their existence continues in the darkness of the Abyss, twisted and fragmented, feeding on the souls of the dead.

For more detailed lore, including the connection to the Darkwraith Covenant, see the Darkwraith Covenant page.
