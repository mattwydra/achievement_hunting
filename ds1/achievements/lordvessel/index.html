Lordvessel

Acquire the Lordvessel.

Hard

Overview

The Lordvessel unlocks fast travel and allows access to the four Lord Soul areas.

To obtain it, you must defeat Ornstein and Smough in Anor Londo — widely considered one of the toughest fights in the game. Afterward, you’ll be rewarded by Gwynevere with the Lordvessel.

Additional Information

Information on boss fight:

  1. Boss Location: Anor Londo, inside the cathedral past the silver knight archers.
  2. Ornstein & Smough Mechanics:
    • One is fast (Ornstein), the other is heavy (Smough). You must fight both at once.
    • Strategy Breakdown:
      • Focus on dodging, not blocking — Ornstein pierces through shields.
      • Pick one target and stick with it — whoever dies second becomes the "super" version. The other will become full health in phase two, no matter what.
      • Super Smough = slower, more damage. Super Ornstein = more aggressive and lightning-based.
      • Use summons (e.g. Solaire) or human phantoms to split aggro.

Lordvessel

Things to know:

  1. You can place the Lordvessel at Firelink Altar by siding with Frampt or Kaathe — this affects lore but not achievement progress.
    • If you don't know who Kaathe is, be careful researching, you may encounter spoilers! I plan to add a useful guide to finding out without spoilers in the future. There are specific 'requirements' that must be met in order to encounter him. If you want to know what they are, see this guide
    • This is a tricky subject, but I almost recommend missing the darkwraith covenant on your first playthrough. In order to 100% the game, you need 3 playthroughs anyway. However, if you really want to get that covenant, there are specific requirements. again, see this guide
  2. Using the Lordvessel enables fast travel between major bonfires.
Note: After the fight, speak with Gwynevere to receive the Lordvessel automatically.
