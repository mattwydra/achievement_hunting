Covenant: Way of White

Discover Way of White Covenant

Easy

Overview

The Way of White Covenant will likely be the first covenant you discover. It is also the easiest covenant to find.

Upon reaching firelink shrine, you will need to locate Petrus in order to complete this achievement. When facing toward the crestfallen warrior in firelink shrine, go through the archway to the right. Continue up the stairs until you see an NPC in a courtyard. Talk to the said NPC, and you will meet Petrus. Speak to him multiple times to exhaust his dialogue. Eventually, he will offer to let you join the Way of White.

NOTE: Accepting the invitation is not necessary for unlocking the achievement

Additional Information

About Petrus of Thorolund:

  • Gives you a Copper Coin if you talk to him after some initial dialogue.
  • Offers basic miracles and items tied to the Way of White playstyle (focused on faith and healing).
  • Appears somewhat aloof, but has more depth to his character as the game progresses.
  • Note: He is a critical part of the Way of White storyline, which focuses on the journey of the headpriestess, Rhea of Thorolund.

Additional Information

About Rhea of Thorolund:

  • Later in the game, you will meet Rhea and her companions behind Petrus (same location).
  • Rhea is headpriestess, and her storyline is closely linked to Petrus's.
  • Rhea will venture into the catacombs with some of her followers. If you are interested in her questline, be sure to locate her there.
  • Note: Her storyline has many steps, many of which are quite complex. I will eventually put together guides for storylines such as hers; will update this later with a link.
