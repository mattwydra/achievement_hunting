Art of Abysswalking

Acquire the Covenant of Artorias ring

Medium

Overview

This achievement is unlocked after defeating Great Grey Wolf Sif and acquiring the Covenant of Artorias ring. This ring is required to traverse the Abyss and fight the Four Kings.

Sif is located in Darkroot Garden, behind the locked gate that can be opened with the Crest of Artorias or through a back path from the Valley of Drakes.

Steps to Acquire the Ring

  1. Purchase the Crest of Artorias from Andre of Astora (20,000 souls) OR take the Valley of Drakes shortcut.
  2. Enter the sealed forest and proceed to the large arena past the forest hunters.
  3. Defeat Great Grey Wolf Sif in battle.
  4. After the battle, you will automatically receive the Covenant of Artorias ring.

Special Lore Note: Saving Sif (DLC)

If you complete the Sanctuary Guardian, Artorias, and Manus boss fights in the Artorias of the Abyss DLC before defeating Sif in the main game, you can rescue a younger Sif trapped behind Manus.

Lore: Artorias and the Abyss

The Covenant of Artorias ring was forged to allow Artorias to enter the Abyss without being consumed by it. Though he ultimately failed, the ring remains as proof of his valiant effort to hold back the darkness. You, the Chosen Undead, must carry on that burden to defeat the Four Kings.
